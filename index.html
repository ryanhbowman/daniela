    International book scouting from the heart of London

    About

    DSLS scouts for the very best books from the UK as well as from other major languages, and advises on adaption into film, TV and audio.

    The agency was founded in London in 2005 and is now scouting for over 50 imprints, international publishing houses and media groups across the world.

    Team

    Daniela began her publishing career at an independent publishing house in Frankfurt, Germany, the home of the book fair. She moved on to join Random House Germany, commissioning German and English language fiction and specialising in writers from the Nordic countries. In 2005, Daniela relocated to London and founded DSLS Ltd.

    Daniela is a member of the London editorial board of New Books in German.
